About the MediClock project:

This project may help blind or old people to remind them about their medicines to eat. They may set
the time they have to eat their medicines so that the alarm may sound at the time they set it. This helps blind people
as it automatically openes the drawer at the respective time thay have set it so that they get to know that they have to
eat their medicine. If the medicine is finished, then the alarm will make a different sound as a
reminder to buy medicine telling them the name of the medicine.
